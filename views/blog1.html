The black hole of information


      In this pandemic situation our education is standing on internet. We all know that internet is a vast suite of information. You can learn any thing from internet but that doesn't mean to learn everything. We are humans and our knowledge has limitations. In this pandemic, big tech companies thinking that a student has to learn more and more and as a student I know how it feels to learn new things. The internet is vast and there is no limitations on information but I think that we forgot how to use information. We are humans and not a hard disk or memory device to store this information on our heads.
         Our education system teaching us on an online mode, and we know it is not an effective technique to learn new things. Recently I take a look at books of small kid's, it contains so much information that a small kid should never able to learn those things in one year of time span and at that early age. The syllabus of this small kids are so huge that a grownup child will require much efforts to learn those things.
       In an online mode exams, we are all aware about how student are giving exam and how they got so much marks. The main question here is that the students are not learning from internet they using it to only get higher grades and education seems worthless because it just becomes a time pass for many students. Here is a catch because what a small kid can also do when a doubt arrived in her mind, He has no one to answer his question and whom he ask. Someone will say to search on internet but as a kid he doesn't understand long paragraphs and the higher English words. The consequence is that he left the study and start playing. No one forced him to sit properly during study, no one say him to do study regularly, no one say him to practice problems of math. This all things leads to country in very bad future.
        As a student I knew that the same condition is happening with everyone and not only with small kids. The humans have one of the feeling called as greed. When a student have greed of learning, he wandered here and there to learn thing and since internet is vast suite of information, the student lost in this blackhole of information and he ending up by not learning anything. Big tech companies want student have to learn new technologies and our college teaching us the technologies of 1980-90's , the questions arrives here is that what a student has to learn and again the student is grinding in both of them. Companies want student have certification by this company or by that company but really a student get time to learn those things. From my personal experience our college thinks that we are at home means we are free all day and it is holiday for us but they forget that we have to attend lectures and we have to do work. Here all the burden of study is pushed on student. The student has to maintain his academic study and also learn new skills for companies. The student are learning from Youtube, from websites and from many other sources and loosing himself in the age of information.
        Please take a look at this problem and think on it. Observe your child and ask him how much problem he is facing, please sit with him and help him in doing his study. Observe yourself and don't be a part of this process. Try to develop wisdom and don't be a memory device.
        Thank you!!!
