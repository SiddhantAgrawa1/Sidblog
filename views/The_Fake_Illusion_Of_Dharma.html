THE FAKE ILLUSION OF DHARMA


        I think many of us heard the word "Dharma" in many holy books such as Mahabharata and Ramayana. But what is the meaning of it. You heard different meaning in different verses of different books and your mind sucks what it actually it is. Lord Krishna explains Dharma to Arjuna in Mahabharata. But it confuses some time, no one can define Dharma clearly. Everyone defines Dharma according to their own way. Today I am going to discuss with you what my thoughts on it, what I discover from it.
        I think Dharma is basically our thoughts or our mentality that we have accepted. I think I can classify Dharma into two types the first one is that whatever thoughts or philosophy a particular group of people accepted it is also called as religion of that people and the second one is what a particular individual accept means its some time its own thoughts or some time it will be someone philosophy. In first one it is broadly accepted that we have to behave like our community people i.e whatever our community people do we have to do same, also we have to do what our religious books said and what our ancestor said but second one is based on individual to itself. Humans have accepted both Dharma.
        But why we require Dharma. Dharma controls man from becoming evil. You have read in every holy book that we always have to do good things, we have to never harm anyone and lot of other things of ethics and morality. Also we have read what happen if we harm someone or do any misbehave, we heard that god will punish for this to us. After many years this thoughts are deeply imbedded in our minds and in a great community of people mind, that will fright everyone to do misbehave. Our minds own judiciary tells ourselves that if we do any misbehave to others he will do same with me. That affreightment controls humans from becoming an evil. I think it is the best part of our first type of Dharma. The second type of Dharma is little bit complicated because we have to find our own Dharma i.e. What I believe. In first type of Dharma lot of good things are written in holy books but not everyone follow them because of second type of Dharma which is related to own individual. What someone thoughts it becomes its own Dharma. No religious books teaches us to harm someone but we sometime show our power to poor people, this is what your religion taught to you. No the first type of Dharma never but the second type of Dharma is.
        Moving towards the point some time people showoff how their Dharma is great both type of and some time their showoff increases to such an extend that they forgot what their Dharma is and fight like an evil. Some people criticize someone for their religion and showing off how my Dharma is superior than your. I think it is an illusion to show how your Dharma is great. Everyone's Dharma is great. I think the real Dharma is one which connect men to men, create humanity towards society, thinks about men's good, help people to create love among them, create an intimacy towards nature, animals and to the everyone. If your Dharma doesn't teaches you this things then please change your second type of Dharma. Do not live in false Dharma, Try to accept everyone as its own beauty. In nature you found both Rose and Lotus blooms, Both have its own beauty, both blooms on completely different places but both are beautiful.
        Thank you!
